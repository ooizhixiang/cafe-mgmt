Cafe Mgmt — Design Directions

Four visual approaches for the Action Feed screen. All use the same design tokens (colors, typography, spacing). The difference is in card style, density, and visual weight.

Direction A: Clean Minimal

White cards with 6px colored left border. Notion-inspired. Maximum whitespace. Cards feel like paper notes pinned to a board. Most restrained option.

Notion aesthetic Border-only color Maximum whitespace Subtle & calm

Comparison Matrix

Side-by-side evaluation of all four directions against the design principles established in previous steps.

A: Clean Minimal

Glanceability⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Color borders work well
Density⭐⭐⭐ — Spacious but limited cards visible
Touch targets⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Generous spacing
Dollar visibility⭐⭐⭐ — Present but not dominant
Calm "all clear"⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Very satisfying empty state
Best forJake — clean, uncluttered, task-focused

B: Colored Tints

Glanceability⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Background tints + border
Density⭐⭐⭐ — Similar to A
Touch targets⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Generous + colored buttons
Dollar visibility⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Colored context helps
Calm "all clear"⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Green tint feels earned
Best forBoth — warmest, most approachable

C: Compact Dense

Glanceability⭐⭐⭐ — Dense but scannable
Density⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Most cards visible
Touch targets⭐⭐⭐ — Tight, needs care
Dollar visibility⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Prominent right-aligned
Calm "all clear"⭐⭐⭐ — Dark bar may feel heavy
Best forDana — overview, data-driven

D: Bold Numbers

Glanceability⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Numbers visible at distance
Density⭐⭐ — Large cards, fewer visible
Touch targets⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Extra-large tap areas
Dollar visibility⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Numbers ARE the card
Calm "all clear"⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Bold completion feels strong
Best forDana — "Show the Money" principle

Recommended: Hybrid A + D

Direction A's clean layout and whitespace with Direction D's number prominence and summary bar. The best of calm minimalism with dollar-first visibility. 6px colored left borders from A + right-aligned bold numbers from D + generous whitespace + direct checklist items on card.

A's layout + whitespace D's number prominence D's summary bar stats A's card border style B's toast pattern
